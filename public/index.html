V E R B A S I Z E R






A B O U T

Verbasizer is a tool for generating random text from a source text. It was inspired by the cut-up technique popularized by William S. Burroughs and Brion Gysin. The cut-up technique involves cutting up a text and rearranging it to create a new text. Verbasizer automates this process by randomly selecting words from a source text and arranging them into new sentences.
